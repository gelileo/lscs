Why learn programming now?

Programming is the language of problem‑solving in today’s world. Learning it early trains the mind to think clearly: break big problems into smaller ones, test ideas, learn from mistakes, and try again. These habits power future learning in computer science, robotics, AI, app development—and in school subjects beyond technology.

Who is this for?

  • Grades 6–8
  • No prior coding experience needed
  • Curious, creative, and ready to build
