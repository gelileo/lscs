Building Design General Description

Project Overview

  • Project Name: New Fosai Mexico Project – Workshop #1
  • Construction Unit: Wuhu Fosai Technology Co., Ltd.
  • Location: Aguascalientes, Mexico
  • Building Area: 29,854.32 square meters
  • Building Structure: Portal frame structure, large scale, designed for a service life of 25 years.
  • Building Floors: Single-story; height from ground to building eave is 15.00 meters.
  • Fire Hazard Classification: Category D, not considered a densely populated workshop. The fire resistance rating of the plant design is level 2.

Design Elevations

  • Elevation indicators are in meters, and general floor plan dimensions are in meters, while construction drawing dimensions are in millimeters.
  • The design zero point (+0.000) is equivalent to an absolute elevation level.
  • Elevation marks for each floor, the roof, and openings are structural elevations.

Wall Construction

  • Below +0.000: Use solid concrete bricks.
  • Exterior Walls: Below 1.200m – 200mm thick hollow brick protective wall; above 1.200m – Profiled steel plate wall.
  • Office Area Partition Walls: 200mm thick hollow bricks with a fire resistance of not less than 2.50 hours. Office and meeting room partitions use lightweight calcium silicate cement boards.
  • Reinforce hollow brick walls in areas requiring load-bearing or perforation, to be confirmed during construction.

Roofing Work

  • Drainage Organization: Refer to the roof plan.
  • Lighting Belt: Use translucent materials capable of self-melting at high temperatures without producing molten drops. The distribution is shown in the roof plan.
  • Roof Type: Corrugated steel roof, detailed in construction methods.
  • Rainwater Gutters: Steel structure eaves with internal gutters using 1.5mm thick 304 stainless steel. Rainwater pipes are 133x95 metal rectangular downspouts.
  • Concrete Roof: Uses an internal rainwater drainage system with DN100 galvanized rainwater pipes.

Door and Window Work

  • All door and window elevations indicate the opening size. Verify dimensions on-site before processing.
  • Except where specified, all exterior windows use dark gray matte aluminum alloy.
  • Frosted glass for bathroom, shower room, and window glass.
  • Gaps between window frames and openings should be filled with polyurethane foam and sealed with sealant.

Decoration Works

  • Selection of decoration materials regarding material, specifications, and color will be provided by the construction unit for approval by the building unit before finalizing samples.
  • Follow the decoration practices shown in the engineering method table.
  • Decoration works should comply with fire safety requirements and not affect structural safety or damage facilities like water, electricity, and ventilation.

Waterproofing Work

  • Bathroom floors and ground have a 1.5mm thick synthetic polymer waterproof coating. The coating should rise along the walls and be at least 200mm high.
  • Bathrooms should have moisture-proof layers with polymer cement waterproof mortar.
  • Pipes passing through floor slabs should have waterproof casings extending at least 20mm above the finished floor.
  • Ensure waterproofing between the floor and wall at the bathroom corners with additional fabric of 300mm width.

Painting Works

  • Use one layer of acrylic primer, followed by the second layer after drying.
  • Apply a layer of 100% acrylic amino paint, 2-3 microns thick, and a second layer after drying.
  • Avoid painting during adverse weather conditions.

Anti-Corrosion and Other Work

  • Sub-components like purlins and tie rods should follow anti-rust methods according to local standards and comply with relevant specifications.
  • All exposed metal parts, except for special mentions, require rust removal to meet SSPC-SP10/NACE No.2 standards, followed by two layers of red lead alkyd anti-rust primer and two layers of gray topcoat.
  • Exposed steel surfaces will use a color confirmed by the construction party.

Construction Notes

  • Combine closely with the professional construction drawings and equipment installation projects during construction.
  • Prohibit using harmful additives and coatings during construction.
  • In case of any errors or discrepancies found in the drawings, report to the designer promptly for resolution.
  • Construction should strictly comply with national standards and ensure cooperation with other specialties like process, structure, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, automation, etc.
  • Maintain strict adherence to quality inspection norms during construction.
